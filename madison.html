Madison Avenue's Buildings

There are a lot of buildings in the Downtown Memphis area that are closed down for business and don't seem to serve a purpose anymore. This has caused many mural artists to flock to the area to add their artwork.

One specific mural project was done by Kyle Taylor in collaboration with Brandon Donahue and the Downtown Memphis Commision. The mural, located on Madison Ave and B.B. King Boulevard, depicts people doing everyday things like getting a haircut and sipping a drink.

At the intersection of Madison and B.B. King Boulevard, these murals are painted on the side of an old skyscraper.
