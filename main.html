Main Street's Civil Rights Mural

On Main Street in Downtown Memphis, you'll find anywhere from delicious and gourmet restaurants, diners, and bars. Trolly cars go up and down the busy street regularly and Beale Street along with the Orpheum Theater are just a couple stops away.

But also on Main Street, you'll notice a massive mural that depicts journalist and activist Ida B. Wells, Black Union soldiers, and a civil rights message. With an array of bold colors and the acknowlegment of the horrors of lynching in the South, this mural serves to make an artistic and political statement.

"Black Lives Matter" is written in small pink letters on a corner of the mural.
